## Workflow

1. Turn a content idea into a hypothesis about audience, topic, format, or
   response.
2. Choose one variable to test.
3. Define metrics before publishing: impressions, engagements, comments, saves,
   sends, profile visits, followers, newsletter subscriptions, or qualified
   conversations.
4. Publish, respond to comments, and collect results after a sensible window.
5. Decide whether to repeat, revise, or stop the content angle.

## Quality Bar

- Do not optimize five variables at once.
- Prefer learning from qualified response over raw reach.
- Treat analytics as directional, not perfect truth.
- Include comment quality and audience fit, not just impressions.
