# LinkedIn Article Newsletter Examples

## Article Outline

```markdown
# [Specific Promise For A Specific Reader]

## The Problem
[What the reader is dealing with and why it matters.]

## The Common Mistake
[What people usually do and why it fails.]

## A Better Way
[Framework, steps, or point of view.]

## Example
[Concrete case, walkthrough, or scenario.]

## What To Do Next
[Practical next step.]

## About The Author
[Short credibility note and relevant profile/CTA.]
```

## Newsletter Edition

```markdown
# [Edition Title]

Opening: [What changed, what question this answers, or why now.]

1. [Insight]
2. [Example]
3. [Action]

One thing to try this week:
[Concrete task]

Reply/comment prompt:
[Question]
```

## "Why Follow Me" Article

```markdown
# Why I Write About [Topic]

I write for [audience] who want to [outcome].

You can expect:
- [recurring topic 1]
- [recurring topic 2]
- [recurring topic 3]

I will not write much about:
- [exclusion]

Start here:
- [best article/post/resource]
```

## Teaser Post For Article

```markdown
I wrote a longer guide on [topic].

It is for [audience] who are trying to [outcome].

The short version:
- [takeaway]
- [takeaway]
- [takeaway]

Full article: [link]
```
