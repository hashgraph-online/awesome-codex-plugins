# LinkedIn Article Newsletter Knowledge

## Core Idea

The book treats articles and newsletters as expertise-building formats. Posts
often get more immediate feed attention, but long-form formats can deepen trust,
explain a point of view, and create a durable asset that can be featured or
promoted through shorter posts.

## Format Decision

| Format | Use When |
|--------|----------|
| Post | The idea can be useful in 3,000 characters or less and needs feed conversation. |
| Article | The idea needs depth, structure, examples, or durable expertise proof. |
| Newsletter | The topic can sustain a recurring promise and the audience benefits from regular editions. |
| Teaser post | The long-form asset needs feed distribution and a comment prompt. |

## Long-Form Jobs

- Explain a method.
- Synthesize industry news.
- Create a "why follow me" or topic manifesto.
- Turn repeated questions into a reference article.
- Convert a high-performing post into a deeper guide.
- Publish a recurring newsletter edition around a narrow professional theme.
