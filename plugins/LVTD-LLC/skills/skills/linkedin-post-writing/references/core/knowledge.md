# LinkedIn Post Writing Knowledge

## Core Idea

The book frames LinkedIn post writing as inbound visibility: useful content
makes the author visible to people who already care about the topic. A strong
post serves a defined audience, reinforces a topic association, and creates a
reason for readers to spend time, comment, or visit the profile.

- Posts have a 3,000 character limit; longer ideas should become articles:
  https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a528176
- Articles can be longer than posts and are limited to 125,000 characters:
  https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a522483
- Members can add alt text to images:
  https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a519856
- Documents can be uploaded and described in a post:
  https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a518909
- Posts can be scheduled within LinkedIn:
  https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1347212
- LinkedIn prohibits fake engagement and tools that manipulate algorithms:
  https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1341387

## The Post Model

1. **Audience value**: the post must answer a problem, question, shift,
   opinion, or story that matters to a specific group.
2. **Topic association**: repeated posts should make the author memorable for a
   small number of themes.
3. **Dwell and readability**: the first lines, structure, media, and document
   format should make the reader stay without tricking them.
4. **Conversation**: the post should make a useful comment easy.
5. **Trust**: reach tactics should not damage reputation or break LinkedIn
   rules.

## Useful Post Jobs

| Job | Best When |
|-----|-----------|
| Interpret news | The author can explain what industry news means for the audience. |
| Teach a lesson | The author has a repeatable method, mistake, or practical example. |
| Build personal brand | The post connects the author with a topic, visual, event, or proof point. |
| Start discussion | A genuine question or tension will improve the topic. |
| Promote deeper content | The post summarizes an article, newsletter, video, or document and gives a reason to open it. |
| Announce | The event, job change, launch, or milestone is tied to reader relevance. |

## Current-Feature Caution

Do not turn old book-specific feature notes into permanent rules. LinkedIn
changes Creator Mode, scheduling, newsletters, video, Live, analytics, and
post formats. If the user asks for operational platform steps, check current
LinkedIn help before giving instructions.
