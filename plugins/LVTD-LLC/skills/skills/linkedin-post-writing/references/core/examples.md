# LinkedIn Post Writing Examples

## Industry News With Interpretation

```markdown
[Observation about the news in one sentence.]

Most teams will read this as [obvious take].

I think the more important shift is [non-obvious implication].

For [audience], that means:
- [consequence 1]
- [consequence 2]
- [action to consider]

The practical question: [discussion prompt]
```

## Personal Lesson

```markdown
I used to think [old belief].

Then [specific experience] changed how I see it.

The mistake was not [surface mistake].
It was [deeper mistake].

Now I use this rule:
[short rule]

Example:
[brief concrete example]

Where have you seen this go wrong?
```

## Document Or Carousel Caption

```markdown
I made a short breakdown of [topic] for [audience].

It covers:
- [slide/page 1 value]
- [slide/page 2 value]
- [slide/page 3 value]

Use it when [situation].

The main idea: [one-sentence takeaway].
```

## Article Teaser

```markdown
I wrote a deeper guide on [topic].

It is for [audience] who are trying to [goal] without [common bad pattern].

The short version:
1. [point]
2. [point]
3. [point]

Full article: [link or LinkedIn article reference]
```

## Rewrite Checklist

- Is the audience obvious?
- Does the first screen say something specific?
- Does the post add interpretation, proof, or practical value?
- Does each paragraph move the thought forward?
- Are hashtags and mentions relevant?
- Is there any fake engagement or spam signal to remove?
