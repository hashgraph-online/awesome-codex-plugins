# Book Sales Optimization Checklist

Use this checklist when auditing a book's purchase path.

## Readiness

- [ ] Book has a clear target reader.
- [ ] Book has evidence of reader value.
- [ ] Book has a discovery path or traffic source.
- [ ] Current platform rules have been verified for specific recommendations.

## Listing Click-Through

- [ ] Cover promise is legible at thumbnail size.
- [ ] Title or subtitle identifies reader outcome.
- [ ] Category and keywords match how readers search.
- [ ] Review count and rating provide enough trust or have a plan to improve authentically.
- [ ] Cover, title, and subtitle work together instead of duplicating weakly.

## Product Page Conversion

- [ ] Description leads with reader situation and outcome.
- [ ] Benefits are specific and skimmable.
- [ ] Reader language from beta feedback or reviews is used.
- [ ] Strong learning outcomes appear as subheads or bullets.
- [ ] Editorial reviews, author profile, samples, and product images are filled where available.
- [ ] Look-inside/sample starts with strong value.

## Reviews

- [ ] Review requests are honest and policy-compliant.
- [ ] No incentives, swaps, fake reviews, or pressure.
- [ ] Early readers, beta readers, and fans know reviews help if they choose to leave one.
- [ ] Current retailer review rules are checked.

## Formats And Boosts

- [ ] Audiobook, direct PDF, additional retailers, bulk sales, translations, and teaching resources are evaluated.
- [ ] Each boost is scored by reach, profit, time, money, maintenance, and stress.
- [ ] No boost is required before launch unless it unlocks a clear opportunity.

## Pricing And Relationship

- [ ] Pricing is not signaling low value.
- [ ] Direct sales are considered if customer relationship value matters.
- [ ] Upsells are complementary and nonessential.
- [ ] Reader contact hooks exist inside the book and product page.

## Red Flags

- The description is only a contents summary.
- The cover is attractive but unclear at thumbnail size.
- The author wants reviews through incentives or manipulation.
- A bonus bundle withholds core book value.
- A platform-specific recommendation is given without checking current rules.
