# Book Sales Optimization Examples

Synthetic examples for post-launch optimization.

## Store Description Rewrite

### Weak

```text
This book covers meetings, agendas, async collaboration, facilitation, and remote work. It includes stories from the author's career and advice for managers.
```

**Problems**:
- Lists topics instead of outcomes.
- Does not identify the reader.
- No concrete promise.

### Stronger

```text
Run remote meetings that end with clear decisions, fewer follow-ups, and less calendar waste.

If you manage a distributed team and your meetings keep turning into status updates, this book gives you a practical reset: when to cancel, when to go async, how to write decision agendas, and how to leave every meeting with one owner per next step.
```

**Why it works**:
- Names a reader situation.
- Leads with a result.
- Lists practical learning outcomes.

## Thumbnail Promise Audit

### Weak

```text
Title: Better Together
Subtitle: Lessons from the modern workplace
Cover: abstract geometric art
```

**Likely issue**: The shopper cannot tell who it is for or what it solves from a small listing.

### Stronger

```text
Title: Remote Meetings That Work
Subtitle: A manager's guide to fewer calls, clearer decisions, and calmer teams
Cover: large readable title, simple meeting/calendar visual
```

**Why it works**:
- Promise remains clear at thumbnail size.
- Reader and outcome are visible.

## Percentage Boost Decision

### Opportunity

Create an online course promoted by a partner with 10,000 semi-relevant subscribers.

### Evaluation

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Reader fit | Medium |
| Revenue | Uncertain |
| Time cost | High |
| Stress cost | Medium |
| Reusable asset | Maybe |
| Decision | Decline or defer unless seed marketing needs it |

**Reasoning**: Extra formats and products are not free. A semi-relevant opportunity can consume time better spent on a clearer bottleneck.

## Upsell Boundary

### Good Complement

The book teaches a complete method. A paid bundle adds editable worksheets, slide templates, and office-hours support.

### Bad Withholding

The book repeatedly says the key templates are available only after buying the bundle.

**Decision rule**:
- If the reader cannot receive the promised value from the book alone, the upsell is weakening trust.
- If the upsell saves time or adds support for motivated readers, it may increase profit without harming recommendability.

## Reader Contact Hook

```text
Inside the book:
"If you use the meeting reset checklist, I would love to hear what happened. Send a note to [address], or download the editable version at [site]."
```

**Why it works**:
- Gives readers a useful reason to make contact.
- Creates feedback and relationship value without withholding the core method.
