## Workflow

1. Identify the original post's claim, audience, and conversation state.
2. Choose a comment job: extend, clarify, add example, respectfully disagree,
   ask a better question, or summarize a useful implication.
3. Write a comment that can stand alone and adds value to the thread.
4. For comments on the user's own posts, reply quickly and keep the conversation
   substantive.
5. Avoid fake, generic, incentivized, or automated engagement.

## Quality Bar

- Add a new thought, example, question, or useful synthesis.
- Make disagreement professional and specific.
- Do not write engagement-bait comments.
- Do not recommend pods, purchased comments, or automated comment tools.
