# Degree Format Summary

Source: `raw/6-学位论文格式（博士）(1).doc`, converted with macOS `textutil` for text inspection.

Verified textual requirements from the standard-format document:

- Paper: A4, 210 mm x 297 mm.
- Print block: 160 mm x 247 mm, excluding header and page-number lines.
- Body: 小四号宋体 for Chinese text; foreign letters and numbers use Times New Roman or a close Word font at the same size.
- Page density: about 30-35 lines per page and 35-38 characters per line.
- Pagination: Arabic page numbers centered at the footer, same size as body text, optionally decorated as `·3·` or `-3-`.
- Header: from the abstract onward; 5 号楷体; left side `东北大学硕士、博士学位论文`, right side chapter number/title.
- First-level chapter headings: 二号黑体, centered, occupying about 3 lines; examples include `第1章 XXX`, `摘要`, `目录`, `参考文献`, `致谢`.
- Second-level headings: 三号黑体, left aligned, occupying about 2 lines.
- Third-level headings: 四号黑体, left aligned, occupying about 2 lines.
- Fourth-level headings: 小四号黑体, left aligned, occupying about 1 line.
- Figure captions: below figures, numbered by chapter such as `图2.1`, caption text in 5 号 font, consistent font throughout.
- Table captions: above tables, centered, numbered by chapter such as `表2.1`, 5 号宋体.
- Equations: centered, numbered by chapter at the right, such as `(2.1)`.
- References: sequential numbering. Examples use `[序号] 作者. 题名[类型]. ...`.

Layout caveat: `textutil` can read and convert the old `.doc`, but its converted DOCX page settings may not preserve the original page metrics. Use the textual requirements above as the binding source and verify final layout in Word or LibreOffice rendering.
